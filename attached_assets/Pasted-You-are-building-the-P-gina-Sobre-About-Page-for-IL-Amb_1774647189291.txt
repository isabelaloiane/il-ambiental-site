You are building the **Página Sobre (About Page)** for **IL Ambiental — Engenharia e Consultoria Ambiental**, a premium environmental engineering and consulting firm based in Pará, Brazil.

## Design Reference
Use the attached screenshot (Kota blog/about page) as the visual layout guide. Key characteristics to replicate:
- Dark forest green hero section with left-aligned headline and a photo card on the right
- Clean white background for the main content area below
- Mixed editorial grid: one large featured block + smaller cards beside it, followed by a 2-column grid
- A dark full-width CTA section before the footer with headline + email/action input
- Structured, multi-column dark footer
- Overall feel: editorial, trustworthy, warm, human — not cold or corporate

## Responsive Behavior
- **Mobile (< 640px):** Hero single column (visual card below text), all grids → single column, mission/vision cards stacked
- **Tablet (640px–1024px):** Top editorial row adapts to single column featured + 2-col below; mission cards 2-col maintained
- **Desktop:** Full layout as described

## Animations (scroll-triggered)
- All sections: fade-up 24px, duration 0.6s, IntersectionObserver threshold 0.15
- Cards: staggered delays (0.08s increments per card)
- Hero: immediate fade-up on load (no scroll trigger)

## Accessibility
- `<h1>` for hero headline
- `<h2>` for section headlines
- Icon containers need aria-hidden="true"
- All cards have semantic structure (heading + paragraph)
- CTA buttons need descriptive aria-labels

## Do NOT
- Use stock photos or AI-generated images
- Use generic gray color scheme — all surfaces must use the forest green palette from global.css
- Make the page feel like a standard corporate "about" page — it should feel editorial and personal
