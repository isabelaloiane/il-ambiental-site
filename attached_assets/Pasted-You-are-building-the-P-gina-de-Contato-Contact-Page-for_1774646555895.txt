You are building the **Página de Contato (Contact Page)** for **IL Ambiental — Engenharia e Consultoria Ambiental**, a premium environmental consulting firm based in Pará, Brazil.

## Design Reference
Use the attached screenshot (Ramp Help Center page) as the visual layout guide. Key characteristics to replicate:
- Full-width dark hero with photographic background, overlay, and a prominent centered headline
- Below the hero: a light card section with headline + two side-by-side action buttons
- Dark-toned category cards in a 2-column grid
- A single wide dark card spanning full width below
- A clean multi-column content grid at the bottom (like "Popular articles")
- Simple, structured footer
- Overall feel: warm, professional, approachable — not cold or bureaucratic

## Responsive Behavior
- **Mobile (< 640px):** Hero text centered and smaller, quick action card full-width no shadow, contact cards stacked, wide card single column, FAQ single column
- **Tablet (640px–1024px):** 2-column FAQ grid, cards maintained
- **Desktop:** Full layout as described

## Accessibility
- Form inputs must have associated `<label>` elements
- Required fields marked with aria-required="true"
- Submit button must have descriptive aria-label
- WhatsApp link opens in new tab with rel="noopener noreferrer"
- Focus styles on all interactive elements

## Notes
- Do NOT use stock photos or placeholder images
- Form submission: on submit, show a success message inside the card ("✓ Mensagem enviada! Nossa equipe responderá em até 24 horas.") — no page reload
- WhatsApp link format: `https://wa.me/55[numero]?text=Olá! Sou da IL Ambiental...`
- All dark cards use the same design system as the hero: forest greens, not generic black/gray
